Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our weather-based recipe suggestion service.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We request your location to provide accurate weather information and relevant recipe suggestions.
  • Dietary Preferences: You may provide dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free) to filter recipes according to your needs.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To suggest recipes based on current weather conditions in your area.
  • To filter recipes according to your dietary preferences.
  • To enhance and personalize your experience.
  • To improve our application and services.

Individual, Societal, and Environmental Considerations

  • We respect your individual privacy and only collect information necessary for core functionality.
  • We promote healthy and inclusive recipe suggestions, considering diverse dietary needs.
  • We encourage environmentally friendly choices by highlighting sustainable and seasonal ingredients when possible.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide core services (e.g., weather data providers). All shared data is anonymized where possible.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Choices

  • You can choose not to share your location, but some features may not work as intended.
  • You can update or remove your dietary preferences at any time.
