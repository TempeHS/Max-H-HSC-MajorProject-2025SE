Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our weather-based recipe suggestion service.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We request your location to provide accurate weather information and relevant recipe suggestions. Location data is only used for this purpose and is not shared with third parties except as required to provide core services.
  • Dietary Preferences: You may provide dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free) to filter recipes according to your needs. This information is stored securely and used solely to personalize your experience.
  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your username, email address, and password. Passwords are securely hashed and emails are encrypted in our database.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data (such as pages visited, features used, and error logs) to improve our services and ensure system reliability.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): If you enable 2FA, we store your TOTP secret in encrypted form to enhance account security.

How We Use Your Information

  • To suggest recipes based on current weather conditions in your area.
  • To filter recipes according to your dietary preferences.
  • To enhance and personalize your experience, including saving favorite recipes and leaving reviews.
  • To improve our application and services through aggregated, anonymous usage analysis.
  • To secure your account and prevent unauthorized access.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide core services (e.g., weather data providers). All shared data is anonymized where possible. We may disclose information if required to comply with legal obligations or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users and the public.

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption of sensitive data (emails, TOTP secrets), password hashing, and secure session management.
  • All data transmissions are protected using HTTPS.
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing are conducted to maintain the integrity of our systems.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can choose not to share your location, but some features may not work as intended.
  • You can update or remove your dietary preferences and account information at any time through your profile settings.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • You can enable or disable two-factor authentication at any time.

Individual, Societal, and Environmental Considerations

  • We respect your individual privacy and only collect information necessary for core functionality.
  • We promote healthy and inclusive recipe suggestions, considering diverse dietary needs and preferences.
  • We encourage environmentally friendly choices by highlighting sustainable and seasonal ingredients when possible.
  • We are committed to digital accessibility and inclusivity for all users.
